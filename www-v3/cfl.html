Centre for Learning

CFL is a small, teacher-run alternative school outside Bangalore.

Rahul went to school here for nearly ten years and this has consequently been a long-term labour of love for him. We combined large, arresting imagery, a colour scheme reminiscent of the local landscape and soft typography to create a website that immediately evokes a sense of the place.
