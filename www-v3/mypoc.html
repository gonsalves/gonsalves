My Piece of Chennai

My Piece of Chennai is a volunteer-run initiative to provide Chennai residents with the necessary information, tools and support to help solve civic issues.

We gave them a new, high-impact colour scheme, an adaptable layout and some suggestions on how best to engage their visitors.
