Introduction – Techshare 2008

I will be speaking at Techshare 2008 (organised by Barrier Break Technologies), the first conference in India looking at issues confronting people with disabilities, and their interactions with technology.

My presentation, titled The Case for Accessibility will attempt to justify some of the reasons for making websites accessible to people with disabilities, looking at both broader philosophical arguments, as well as practical methods in which websites can be made easily accessible.

Any assistance towards my expenses – travel and registration specifically – would be most welcome. Email me at desk@rahulgonsalves.com if you would like to help out, or visit the Sponsorship page for more details.

Rahul Gonsalves, Spring 2008.
