Techshare 2008

I will be speaking, at Techshare 2008 (organised by Barrier Break Technologies), the first conference in India looking at issues confronting people with disabilities, and their interactions with technology.

My presentation will attempt to justify some of the reasons for making websites accessible to people with disabilities, looking at both broader philosophical arguments, as well as practical methods in which websites can be made easily accessible.

Any assistance towards my expenses – travel and registration specifically – would be most welcome. Email me, at desk@rahulgonsalves.com, if you would like to help out, or if you would like further details.

Rahul Gonsalves, Spring 2008.
